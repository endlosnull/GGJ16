Pitz Blitz is a game lost in time from an ancient civilization. You will use WASD and JK or a Gamepad
Score a goal by throwing a ball through the hoop. But not your side, from the opposite side! The arrow will guide you.
Customize your moves by inputing a sequence mapped to J, and a different sequence mapped to K. On gamepads it is A and B.
